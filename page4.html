Project: ESP32-C3-Zero Case

Final Design

The final design of the case was a significant improvement over the previous prototypes. It featured a more compact and sleek design, with better ventilation and improved durability. The chip was securely positioned inside the case, with all pins properly enclosed. The final design also included additional features such as mounting holes for easy installation and a more user-friendly interface for accessing the chip's ports.

Final Improvements

Area Improvement
Pin Enclosure All pins fully enclosed; tolerances adjusted to prevent exposure and shorts.
Lid & Snap Fit Redesigned snap geometry for easier press, secure closure, and button alignment.
Ventilation Vent holes repositioned to avoid lid coverage and improve convection cooling.
Mounting & Access Added mounting holes and a more user-friendly port access layout.
