Project: ESP32-C3-Zero Case

First Prototype

The First Prototype This cover is designed to provide robust protection for the ESP32-C3-Zero development board. 3D-printed using durable PLA material, it features a two-piece design that snaps together around the board. It includes a dedicated opening for a USB-C port but lacks other ports and has several drawbacks.
